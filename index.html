How LexSheet works
Simple for visitors and straightforward for admins.

Visitors answer a few prompts to see estimated closing costs. Administrators control licenses, domains, and firm-specific settings so the experience remains accurate over time.

Visitor flow

  • Client enters basic transaction details and optional lender info.
  • The calculator returns estimated totals with a printable breakdown.
  • Your contact information and disclaimers stay front and center.

Admin controls

  • License-level branding, phone, and schedule updates.
  • Domain allowlists for each installation.
  • Support for additional licenses as your footprint grows.
